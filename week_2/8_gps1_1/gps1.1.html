Checking in

Good pairing sessions feel like a conversation. Good communication during pairing is mainly through vocalizing your thoughts and thought process. Check in with your pair, ask them how their day is going and what state of mind they're in.

Driving

The Driver should never be looking at a line of code they are not told to look at by the Navigator. The driver presses the gas, but the navigator says when to turn.

Navigating

The Navigator should always describe what code they are looking at by the line number.
